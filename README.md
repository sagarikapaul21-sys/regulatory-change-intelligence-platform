# MediSure AI-Powered Insurance Intelligence Platform

## Overview

MediSure is an AI-powered insurance intelligence platform designed to streamline information retrieval, knowledge management, and decision-support processes using Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), vector search, and workflow automation.

The solution demonstrates how AI can be integrated with business workflows to provide contextual responses, automate information discovery, and improve operational efficiency.

---

## Business Problem

Insurance and healthcare organizations manage large volumes of documents, policy information, guidelines, and customer queries.

Manual review and information retrieval can lead to:

* Delayed response times
* Repetitive manual effort
* Knowledge silos
* Inconsistent information delivery
* Reduced operational efficiency

---

## Solution

MediSure leverages AI and workflow automation to:

* Retrieve relevant information from a vector database
* Perform semantic search across knowledge sources
* Generate context-aware responses
* Summarize complex information
* Support business decision-making workflows
* Automate information processing tasks

---

## Technology Stack

* n8n Workflow Automation
* OpenAI GPT Models
* Pinecone Vector Database
* Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
* Embeddings-Based Search
* JSON Workflow Orchestration
